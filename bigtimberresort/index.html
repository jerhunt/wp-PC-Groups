The Resort

A Mountain Retreat Like No Other

Nestled just minutes from Red River Gorge and Natural Bridge State Park in Jeffersonville, Kentucky, Big Timber Resort is a secluded cabin development set across acres of old-growth forest inside Daniel Boone National Forest. Each cabin is thoughtfully crafted to blend rugged authenticity with modern comfort — giving guests a true backcountry experience without sacrificing the finer things.

Whether you're planning a romantic couples getaway, a hiking basecamp for exploring Red River Gorge, or simply a quiet retreat deep in the Kentucky wilderness — Big Timber has a cabin designed for your stay.

Getting Here

Big Timber Resort is located in Jeffersonville, Kentucky — just minutes from the entrance to Red River Gorge, Natural Bridge State Park, and the trails of Daniel Boone National Forest. The scenic drive through the Kentucky hills is part of the experience.
